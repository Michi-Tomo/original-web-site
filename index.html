Michi

I was born in Hyogo Prefecture in 1979.
I love traveling, photography, and fashion.
I experienced my first trip abroad when I was 20 years old.
I was stunned by the night view from the skyscrapers in Chicago at that time, and it made me decide to travel for the rest of my life.
I want to keep traveling around the world, seeing fashion from all over the world, and taking plenty of photos.
